About Lau

The Lau Group

Yasana ko Lau stretches across the eastern waters of Fiji, joining island communities through chiefly leadership, kinship, faith, maritime skill, and careful stewardship of land and sea.

{% include "partials/masi_divider.html" %}

Geography

The Lau islands include Oneata, Moce, Kabara, Fulaga, Ogea, Vatoa, Ono-i-Lau, Cicia, Nayau, Vanuabalavu, Lakeba, and surrounding island communities.

Culture

Masi, tabua, tokatoka, mataqali, yavusa, and vanua relationships shape identity, ceremony, accountability, and community care.

Leadership

The Roko Tui Lau and Provincial Office coordinate public administration with village, tikina, and yavusa leaders throughout Lau.
